This is the bar chart

This bar chart shows how maximum of frp changes in January 2020. FRP is the abbreviation of Fire Radiative Power, which is the rate of emitted radiative energy by the fire at the time of the observation. Units: Burned Area is expressed in units of area such as km2.

This the the map to show the fire radiative power in one day. And obviously, the fire radiative power near Sydney and Canberra is the most serious part. Lots of bush fire with high frp value occured.
